Fresh-From-the-Garden Caprese

Description

I picked a pretty easy recipe to add here. The image is from when I had fresh tomatoes and basil straight from my garden and so then I bought mozarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh mozarella
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Slize the tomatoes
  2. Lay the tomato slices on a plate
  3. Lay basil and mozarella over the tomatoes
  4. Drizzle everything with olive oil
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
