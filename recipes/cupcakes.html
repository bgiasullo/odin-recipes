Patriotic Cupcakes

Description

These were fun and looked really cute at my 4th of July party.

Ingredients

  • Box of cake mix
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Red cake-decorating liquid from a squirt bottle
  • Fresh blueberries
  • A sense of humor

Steps

  1. Make the cupcakes according to the instructions on the cake mix box
  2. When the cupcakes are cooled, frost them with vanilla frosting
  3. Drizzle the red decorating liquid over the cupcakes. Make some fun designs. Go wild. Freedom.
  4. Place blueberries on the cupcakes however you damn please
